Welcome

Hi, I'm Adrian! I'm currently a graduate student at the University of Michigan, studying Health Informatics. My program aims to explore the intersection of health, technology, and UX research & design. Through my studies, I hope to design and test health tools as a means of empowering low socioeconomic communities and optimizing clinical flow within healthcare systems. In my spare time, I love to enjoy a cup of pour-over coffee, reflect on life, or go for a run around the neighborhood.

My Design Philosophy: 細心 (literal: precise heart) is a Chinese idiom that refers to an awareness to the smallest details. My desire is to create user-centered tools with empathy and thoughtfulness in mind.
